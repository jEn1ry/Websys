Adobo

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Chicken 2 1/2 lbs.
Chicken cube 1 knorr
Garlic 1 head.
Onion 1 pcs.
Brown sugar 1 1/2 teaspoon
Bay leaves 4 pcs.
Whole peppercorn 1 1/2 tsp.
Soy sauce 4 tbsp
White vinegar 6 tbsp.
Water 1 cup.
Green onions 1/4 cup.
Butter 1 tbsp.
Cooking oil 3 tbsp.

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, and half of the crushed garlic in a bowl. Mix until all ingreadients are well blended. Let it stay for at least 1 hour.
  2. Fry the reamining garlic until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Combine the butter and garlic-infused oil (this is the oil used to cook the garlic). Saut√© the onion and then add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken turns light brown.
  4. Add the oyster sauce and then pour the remaining marinade and water. Let it boil.
  5. Add Knorr chicken cube, sugar, bay leaves, fried garlic, and whole peppercorn. Reduce to a simmer. Cover the pot and cook for 35 to 45 minutes.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl. Garnish with chopped green onions.
  7. Serve. Share and enjoy!
